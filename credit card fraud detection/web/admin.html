CREDIT CARD
Fraud Detection


Due to rise and rapid growth of E-commerce,use of credit cards for online purchase has dramatically increased and it caused an explosion in the credit card fraud.As credit card becomes the most popular mode of payment for the both online as well as regular purchase,cases of fraud associated with it are also rising.Many modern techniques based on Artificial Intelligence,Data mining ,Fuzzy logic ,Machine learning, Sequence Alingment, Generic Programming etc.,has evolved in detecting various credit card fraudulent transactions.
